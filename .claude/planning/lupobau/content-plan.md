## Translation Guidelines (German)

### Tone & Voice

**Professional and Friendly**:
- Use "wir/unser" (inclusive)
- Formal "Sie" address
- Short sentences (10-20 words)
- Active voice: "Wir bauen" vs "wird gebaut"

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Unser Team realisiert Ihr Projekt mit Sorgfalt"
- ❌ "Projekte werden realisiert"

### Keyword Integration

**Natural Integration**:
- Primary: H1, first paragraph, last paragraph
- Secondary: Throughout body
- LSI: Replace repetition

**Density Limits**:
- Primary: Max 1.5%
- Secondary: Max 1%
